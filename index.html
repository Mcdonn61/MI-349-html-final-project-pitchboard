Title


Off The Backboard Highlights


Short Pitch


A website where basketball fans can go and checkout the latest highlights (and lowlights) from the basketball world.


Long Pitch


When we were planning this website, we had one motive in mind, bringing basketball junkies together. Off The Backboard Highlights will have everything that basketball fans are into. The website will be broken off into multiple different pages with each page highlighting what is going on with the topic of the page. The three main pages after the homepage are NBA, WNBA, and College Basketball. On each of those three pages there will be everything from videos of highlights from players, teams, and games, as well as top stories, gambling advice, and lowlights, and much more. After those three main pages, there will be pages for the top stories, gambling advice, highlights and lowlights for all of basketball. Off The Backboard Highlights will become a destination for NBA fans, College Basketball fans, and people that gamble on basketball.


Personas


  • Casual Basketball fan looking for the latest news.
  • Sports gambler looking for advice on bets.
  • Basketball player looking for highlights of his/her favorite player(s).


Comps
